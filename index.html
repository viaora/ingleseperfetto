Welcome to InglesePerfetto!

My name is Mauro Marion Bizzotto.
I am an experienced tutor and the founder of InglesePerfetto. The prime focus of my work is child development.

At its core InglesePerfetto provides learning for an array of subjects:

路 English language for native or non-native speakers 路 French language 路 Drum set, music preliminary instruction (piano, guitar)
路 Music technology

InglesePerfetto combines interdisciplinary learning which aims to stimulate and encourage independent thinking and reasoning. Rather than merely delivering sterile instruction, I like to motivate children to explore and learn why and how reality and things work the way they do.

About my education background and credentials:

I am a graduate of Trinity Laban Conservatoire of Music and Dance, London UK, and the recipient of two awards bestowed by Berklee College of Music Faculty, Boston MA - USA, and a certified English teacher - CELTA, Cambridge University UK.

About my work experience:

I have been teaching music since 2002 and English language since 2017 - Over the course of my teaching career I have worked in both the public and private education sectors. Since the inception of InglesePerfetto, in 2018, I have been working as an independent educator.

About me:

I grew up in Italy. I left Bassano del Grappa, my home town, in spring of 1997 and then relocated to London,UK - In summer of 2021 I moved back to Italy.

  • I am fully bilingual - Italian/English (neutral accent)
  • I also speak French (full professional proficiency)
  • I am a musician and an aspiring music producer.
