Welcome to InglesePerfetto!

My name is Mauro Marion Bizzotto.

I am a knowledgeable and passionate educator specializing in child development and study skills for young learners. My goal is to enhance a child's ability to study, understand and recall information effectively and at will.

At its core InglesePerfetto combines interdisciplinary learning which aims to stimulate and encourage independent thinking and reasoning.

InglesePerfetto provides functional learning for:

  • English and Italian literacy, elocution, diction
    for the development of effective communication skills, creative thinking, emotional intelligence and social skills
  • Numeracy and math
    for the development of reasoning skills, problem solving, lateral thinking
  • Drum set instruction and piano-preliminary instruction
    for the development of fine motor skills, overall coordination, reasoning skills, problem solving, lateral thinking, emotional intelligence, self-awareness and spirituality

About my education background and credentials:

  • I am a certified English teacher - CELTA, Cambridge University UK, 2017
  • I am a former graduate of Trinity Laban Conservatoire of Music and Dance, London UK, BMus (Hons) Jazz Performance - Class of 2012
  • I am the recipient of two music awards bestowed by Berklee College of Music Faculty, Boston USA - 2004, 2008 Umbria Jazz Clinics
About my work experience:

I taught English foreign language to international students and worked with British School Bassano, Scuola Primaria San Giuseppe Bassano, Angle Education London, St. Andrews College Glasgow. I started InglesePerfetto in 2018, following a burning desire to act as an independent educator and focus mainly on child development. My aim is to help families maximize their children's potential for achievement in life - be that in academia, work and business, sport and personal growth. Rather than merely delivering sterile instruction, I like to motivate children to explore and learn why and how reality and things work the way they do. Unbiased, effective reasoning and strong communication skills are the fulcrum of my teachings.

About me:

I was born in Bassano del Grappa, a beautiful town in the Veneto region - Italy. I left Bassano in spring of 1997 and relocated to London UK, in order to pursue music as a career (I am a drummer). I have been living in London UK for 17 years now. I also lived in New York for about 3 years - from summer of 2000 to summer of 2003. Besides my passion for music and education, I have a keen interest in behavioral psychology and the capital markets. I love cycling, running, hiking and swimming. I live, work in Italy and the UK.
