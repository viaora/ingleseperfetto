My name is Mauro Marion Bizzotto.

I am the founder of InglesePerfetto.

InglesePerfetto is a tuition service focused on helping students acquire the necessary skills to become confident speakers of the English language

InglesePerfetto specializes in:

  • Cambridge Exams Preparation (B1, B2, C1, C2) and IELTS (General, Academic).
    We help international students prepare for the Cambridge examination test and the IELTS test. We endeavor to help our students overcome their difficulties with the language and to boost their confidence, so when the time to sit the exam comes, they know exactly how to approach it
  • Business English
    We help our clients improve their speaking and communication skills with a particular focus on business, finance and current affairs. More specifically, we aim to facilitate our clients' understanding of the British and American culture. The deeper and the more subtle an individual's understanding of the British and American culture, the greater his/her opportunity to initiate meaningful working, business relationships with professionals or other entities from the USA, UK, Common-Wealth countries and the rest of the world
  • English For Adults
    This program is for English language enthusiasts and individuals who wish to refine and solidify their existing listening and speaking abilities. The course will allow participants to expand their vocabulary, learn countless idiomatic expressions, improve their pronunciation and explore British, American culture
  • English For Children
    These are fun and ingenious English lessons aimed at improving your child's self-awareness, self-esteem, confidence and understanding of reality - An authentic and imaginative learning- journey inspired by the teachings of Dr Tony Buzan and Dr David Eagleman

About my work experience and teaching philosophy:

I have been teaching English foreign language to children, international students and business professionals since 2017. I have worked in both the public and the private sectors in Italy and the UK.
I started my own English Foreign Language Laboratory “InglesePerfetto” in 2018, following my desire to act as an independent educator. My goal is to help students maximize their own potential for achievement in life - be that in academia, work, business, sport, etc. Unbiased, effective reasoning and strong communication skills are at the very core of my own teaching philosophy. Most importantly my lessons and activities are based on information that finds application in real life situations. Overall I see my work as an opportunity to inspire, guide, encourage, build confidence and promote self-esteem in children, teenagers and adults.
